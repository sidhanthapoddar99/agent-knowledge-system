# Naming the "process" section

The section that holds active deliberation — ideation, research, exploration,
weighing options — needs a name that contrasts with **Notes** (the finalized output).
Candidates considered:

- **Explore** — broadest fit; research + exploration + ideation all read as
  "exploring". Verb-active, pairs cleanly with Notes.
- **Discovery** — the standard product term for the research→explore→decide phase.
  Strong on research, fine for ideation.
- **Workshop** — "where ideas get worked out"; broad but a touch jargony.
- **Brainstorm** — punchy and clear; skews toward ideation, slightly under-signals
  the research/exploration content.

**Decision: Brainstorm.** It read best despite the ideation skew — the research /
exploration content is covered by convention (the section's charter), and the word is
the most immediately legible to a human or agent landing in the issue.

This file is itself the worked example: *this deliberation* is brainstorm; the decided
structure graduates into `notes/`.
